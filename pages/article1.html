Wealthy Man Experiences Discomfort When Notices The Toiler Paper Dispener Has Run Out

Vadym Prokopov 8:32 PM

WEST VILLAGE, NY—In what some folks usually call a minor discomfort, Michael Morrison, a 35 year old male living in a sumptuous penthouse had felt discomfort due to not being able to wipe his buttocks after using the loo. First hand reports say that the man had woken up at 3:23 PM after taking a 2 hour nap at his balcony with a 360 degree view of the Hudson river and decided to go to the restroom to "get rid" of his brunch which consisted of: a Bavarian puffed pancake made with Granny Smith apples and topped with whipped cream and three waffle Dogs—breakfast sausages on sticks, dipped in waffle batter and fried to a golden brown. Mr. Morrison has given us the following quote when he was asked what had happened:

"I woke up that day and decided to go the restroom. After taking a restroom break, as I was standing up I had realized that my automatic toilet dispenser had run of toilet paper. I had felt completely terrified when this realization had occured on me. I had to actually raise my voice to have my manservant come and replace the toilet paper in the dispenser since I had never done it before myself. I had never felt a greater discomfort in my life than at that moment."

Our reporters were able to get a quote from the manservant that had to refill the toilet paper dispenser:

"I was on the phone with my wife (he is a father of 5) deciding whether we can afford to send our 2nd oldest child to school as I heard Mr. Morrison yell at me from the bathroom: "WHY IS THERE NO TOILET PAPER?" as I hurried towards the 3rd floor restroom, he continued to yell insults at me such as "YOU MAGGOT CAN'T EVEN PUT THE TOILET PAPER IN PROPERLY CAN YOU, THIS IS WHY YOU GET PAID WHAT YOU GET PAID YOU BAFOON", as I continued to make my way up the stairs from the first floor he began to yell profanities at me. When I had made it to the restroom in which he was I had refilled the toilet paper dispenser as fast as I physically could and backed up as Mr. Morrison came out of the restroom and told me that he is not paying me for this week of work."

After hearing that our reporters decided to contacted the manservant's wife and see her side of the story:

"My husband came home with a distraught face as if he was about to cry, I couldn't tell what had happened until he told me about his day after cooking dinner for our family of seven out of just one can of beans. Our son is most likely unable to go to elemntary school this year due to financial status. We can barely afford paying the rent to our 2 bedroom apartment since I was fired from my job since my employer said that I could not work for him due to my family background being in a poor region and having an unstable economical state due to the varying pays my husband gets. He apologized greatly and told me that it wasn't my fault that the school I went to didn't have good education due to the poor funding of the neighbourhood caused by systematic oppression."

Now, after hearing the story from the wife we had went back to Michael to see what he was up to after this tragic event for him. We were unable to get a statement from Michael since he was too busy playing video games with his brother. As far as our reporters can tell, we can conclude that Michael had went back to living his life like normal even though he had been through a tragic event, his servant had been fired and was kicked out of his apartment by the landlord for 2 months overdue rent. Life had returned back to normal in Michael's penthouse when he hired a new manservant.
