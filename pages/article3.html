Vadym Prokopov 8:32 PM

Global warming. A problem regarded by many as the suicide of humanity. Global warming is regarded as this problem which only us humans can solve but in my opinion, I believe that global warming isn't as bad of a thing as people say it is. I feel like global warming could actually serve us some good. When the oceans rise due to an increase in the amount of water due to melted icebergs countries all around the world (especially the US) are going to begin building giant walls to stop the water from completely devastating cities. People who will own the coastland will be able to earn huge amounts of profit due to the government wanting to buy their property to build a wall on it. By building this wall, many new industrial jobs will be created which will allow the US as a country the prosper economically since unemployment rates will go down and income inequality between all people will slowly decrease. Global warming is regarded as a bad thing when clearly it isn't. People make the argument that we need to lower carbon emissions produced by factories by creating a large carbon tax but all that would do is for us, consumers, to pay more for driving our cars and force some factories to shut down due to their carbon emission levels. This would cause a phenomenally large decrease in employment, average income, and other economic factors. Some countries in our world may be unable to fund huge projects such as constructing a giant wall to stop water, this will cause the entire countries to flood which will provide the ecosystem with more ground to cover and will actually increase the entropy of earth as a system and allow life to prosper under water. This will in a way reverse the effects of humans on the ecosystem and reverse the fact that humans have been exploiting the ecosystem on a daily basis and destroying the beautiful nature around us.
